Bangladesh Successfully Launches Second Satellite into Orbit

Date: 05/01/2025 Time: 10.25 PM

Bangladesh has launched its second satellite, "Bangabandhu-2," into orbit, marking another milestone in the country’s space ambitions. The satellite aims to enhance telecommunications, internet connectivity, and disaster management capabilities. This launch solidifies Bangladesh's position in the global space community and promises to bridge the digital divide across rural and urban areas.
